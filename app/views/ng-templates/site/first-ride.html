How to book a ride
1

Book Online

Log in to your Electric Studio account to buy a package and book a ride. For your first time, we recommend buying the First Timer Package so you can try as many instructors and rides in 2 weeks.

Select a package
2

All you need

CLOTHING

We suggest from-fitting sportswear. For ladies, we recommend leggings. Bring a change of clothes since your sure to sweat during class!

SHOES

We will provide cycling shoes. If you have your own, make sure they are Look Keo compatible.

WATER

We have water available for sale, but we've got fountains for you to fill up your own bottles.

AMENITIES

Our lockers have coded keypads, so leave your lock behind. We provide complimentary towels, bath and body products so you can shower and head straight to where you need to be right after class.

3

SAY HELLO

Get the most out of your Electric experience and arrive 30 minutes before your ride. We will provide you with shoes, set up your bike and gibe you a tour of our studio. If you do not check in 5 minutes before class, your reserved bike may be released.
