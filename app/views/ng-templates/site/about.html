About Us

We are the first premier indoor cycling boutique offering a full body fitness experience. Founded by an innovative team of fitness professionals set on revolutionising the fitness culture in the Philippines, our rides are the ultimate sweat session - an energising workout to challenge you physically and strengthen you mentally

Turn It Up

The Electric Experience is a full body workout focused on optimal movements, music and motivation. We use to choreograph to strengthen the core and use weights to tone the upper body. We believe fitness switches everything on - your body, your mind and how you live your life.

Move as a Pack

We ride in chorus, leaving no one behind. We are a community surrounded by high energy beats, support and positivity. We believe we are great on our own but stronger together.

Strive for Super

Our inspirational instructors and candlelit experience allow our riders to clear their minds and strengthen their spirit. Each class motivates riders to find their power and push beyond their comfort zones. we believe positive change is contagious - what good you do for yourself, you do for others.
